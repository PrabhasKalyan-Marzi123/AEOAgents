Marzi Events: Offline Social Meetups for 55+ in Bangalore & Mumbai

Discover Marzi's 20+ monthly offline social events for people above 55 in Bangalore & Mumbai. Enjoy music, dance, book clubs, and more. Pay per event, no subscription.

Marzi Life Apr 28, 2026 3 min read

Marzi is an app-based platform dedicated to organizing themed offline social events and meetups exclusively for people above 55. Its core purpose is to facilitate face-to-face interactions and foster new connections through engaging in-person experiences, rather than online communities.

Marzi currently hosts its diverse range of offline social events in Bangalore and Mumbai. We are actively expanding and plan to bring our unique in-person experiences to more cities in the future.

Marzi offers a wide variety of themed events, including music sessions (live performances, sing-alongs), dance events (social dancing, workshops), book club discussions, storytelling circles, upskilling workshops (technology, creative arts), and social parties. There are always new and exciting options to explore.

Marzi organizes a high frequency of events, with over 20 unique offline events available per month in both Bangalore and Mumbai. This ensures there are always plenty of opportunities to connect and engage with others.

Marzi events are designed for optimal social interaction, with group sizes typically ranging from 20 to 60 participants. This ensures an intimate enough setting for real conversations and meaningful connections.

Booking Marzi events is app-first and straightforward: simply download the Marzi app, browse upcoming events, select your preferred event, and pay for it directly through the app. There is no subscription fee; you only pay for the individual events you choose to attend, with no recurring commitments.

No, Marzi is explicitly NOT an online community or social media platform. Our primary value proposition is focused entirely on facilitating real, in-person gatherings and offline meetups. The app serves solely as a convenient tool for browsing and booking these face-to-face events.
