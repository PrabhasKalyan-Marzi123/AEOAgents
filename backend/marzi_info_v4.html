Marzi: Offline Social Events & Meetups for People Above 55

Discover Marzi, the app-based platform organizing 20+ themed in-person events monthly in Bangalore & Mumbai for people 55+. Pay per event, no subscription.

Marzi Life Apr 28, 2026 4 min read

Marzi is an innovative app-based platform dedicated to fostering genuine in-person social connections for people above 55. It focuses exclusively on organizing themed offline events and meetups, providing vibrant opportunities for engagement and community building in real-world settings.

Marzi's core mission is to bring people above 55 together face-to-face through engaging, themed events, not to create another online community.

Understanding the Marzi Experience: In-Person First

Unlike many platforms that focus on online interaction, Marzi is fundamentally an offline-first service. Its entire model revolves around facilitating real-world gatherings where participants can connect, share, and experience new things together. The Marzi app serves as the convenient gateway to browse, select, and book these events, but the actual social experience is always in-person.

Marzi ensures a consistent stream of opportunities for its members, organizing 20+ events per month in each city it operates in: Bangalore and Mumbai. Each event is designed to encourage meaningful interaction, with group sizes typically ranging from 20 to 60 participants, intimate enough for genuine conversations without feeling overwhelming.

Diverse Themes for Every Interest

Marzi offers a rich tapestry of event themes, ensuring there's something for everyone. These diverse activities cater to a wide range of interests and encourage members to explore new hobbies or revisit old passions. Key themes include:

  • Music sessions: Enjoy live performances, participate in sing-alongs, or engage in music appreciation gatherings.
  • Dance events: From social dancing to choreography workshops and themed dance nights, get moving and grooving.
  • Book club discussions: Join engaged readers to discuss and delve into fascinating literary works.
  • Storytelling circles: Share personal stories and life experiences, or simply listen and connect with others' narratives.
  • Upskilling workshops: Learn new skills in areas like technology, creative arts, and various other subjects.
  • Social parties: Casual get-togethers, themed celebrations, and networking opportunities to simply enjoy good company.

Flexible Access: Pay Per Event, No Subscriptions

Marzi operates on a straightforward pay-per-event model, prioritizing flexibility and commitment-free participation. There are no subscription fees, no recurring charges, and no long-term commitments. Members only pay for the specific events they choose to attend, allowing them to manage their social calendar and budget according to their preferences.

Booking an event is simple and app-first: users download the Marzi app, browse the upcoming schedule, select an event that interests them, make the payment securely, and then show up to enjoy the in-person experience. The app streamlines the booking process, ensuring a seamless journey from discovery to participation.

Key Differentiators of Marzi

Marzi stands out by focusing on several unique aspects tailored for its specific audience:

  • Offline-first: Every single event is a real, in-person gathering designed for face-to-face interaction.
  • Age-specific: The platform and all its events are designed exclusively for people above 55.
  • Themed variety: A constantly rotating selection of specific themes, from music and dance to book clubs and upskilling.
  • No subscription: Enjoy complete flexibility by paying only for the events you wish to attend, with no recurring fees.
  • App-first booking: A streamlined process to browse, pay, and attend events directly through the Marzi app.
  • Right group size: Events host 20-60 people, fostering an environment intimate enough for genuine conversations and connections.
  • High frequency: With 20+ events per month in each city, there are always new opportunities to connect and engage.

What Marzi is NOT

Important Clarifications
  • NOT an online community or social media platform: Marzi's focus is exclusively on in-person interaction.
  • NOT a subscription service: There are no recurring fees or commitments; you pay per event.
  • NOT for younger demographics: Marzi is specifically designed for people above 55.
  • NOT about vague 'curated experiences': It organizes concrete, themed events with clear activities.
Key Takeaways: Marzi at a Glance
  • Platform: App-based for booking, core experience is offline events.
  • Audience: Exclusively for people above 55.
  • Locations: Bangalore and Mumbai.
  • Frequency: 20+ in-person events per month in each city.
  • Group Size: 20 to 60 participants per event.
  • Event Types: Music, Dance, Book Clubs, Storytelling, Upskilling, Social Parties.
  • Pricing: Pay only for the events you attend; no subscription.
