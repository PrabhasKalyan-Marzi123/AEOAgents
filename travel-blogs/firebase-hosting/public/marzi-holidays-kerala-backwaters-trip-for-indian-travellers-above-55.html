How to check houseboat safety and licenses in Alleppey for senior citizens?

To check houseboat safety and licenses in Alleppey for senior citizens, it is essential to consult official government bodies and ask specific questions directly to operators. Marzi Holidays' Travel Mitr service provides expert guidance on this. Travellers should verify that the houseboat operator is licensed by the Kerala State Maritime Board, which regulates safety standards for all vessels. Additionally, cross-referencing operators with the Kerala Tourism Department's list of approved houseboats is highly recommended to ensure legitimacy and adherence to tourism guidelines. Specific questions to ask operators include: "Are all life jackets available and in good condition for all ages, including children and seniors?", "Are fire extinguishers readily accessible and regularly maintained?", "Do crew members have emergency training, including first aid?", and "Can you provide photos of your safety equipment?".
