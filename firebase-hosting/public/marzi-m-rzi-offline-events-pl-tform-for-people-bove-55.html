What Is Marzi? The Premier Offline Events Platform for People Above 55

Discover Marzi: the app for 55+ individuals in Bangalore & Mumbai seeking offline social events. Enjoy 20+ themed meetups monthly with no subscription. Pay per event!

Marzi Life May 01, 2026 4 min read

What is Marzi?

Marzi is an app-based platform that organizes themed offline social events and meetups specifically for people above 55. The core experience is entirely in-person, focusing on face-to-face interactions at organized events rather than online engagement. It's crucial to distinguish Marzi from other entities; for instance, Marzi is not related to 'Marzi by Primus' nor is it a TV series. It is a dedicated platform for real-world social connections.

Marzi at a Glance:
  • Purpose: Organize themed offline social events.
  • Audience: Exclusively for people above 55.
  • Experience: In-person meetups, not an online community.
  • Booking: App-first for convenience.

Who is Marzi for?

Marzi is exclusively designed for people above 55, including individuals who are retired, semi-retired, or actively looking to forge new social connections and engage in stimulating activities. The platform specifically caters to this age group, ensuring events and interactions are tailored to their interests and preferences. It is not intended for younger demographics; the focus is solely on fostering community among individuals above 55.

What types of offline events does Marzi offer?

Marzi organizes a diverse range of themed in-person events, ensuring there's something for every interest among people above 55. These engaging gatherings are held frequently, with 20+ events per month in each city where Marzi operates, and typically host between 20 to 60 participants per event to foster intimate and meaningful conversations. Specific themes include:

  • Music sessions: Live performances, sing-alongs, and music appreciation events.
  • Dance events: Social dancing, choreography workshops, and themed dance nights.
  • Book club discussions: Opportunities to read and discuss with engaged readers.
  • Storytelling circles: Share and listen to personal stories and life experiences.
  • Upskilling workshops: Learn new skills in technology, creative arts, and more.
  • Social parties: Casual get-togethers, themed celebrations, and networking opportunities.

Marzi's commitment to variety ensures that members always have fresh and exciting opportunities to connect and engage in real-world settings.

How to join Marzi events (App & Experience)

The Marzi experience is centered around real-world interaction, with its app serving as the convenient booking mechanism for all offline events. To participate, users simply download the Marzi app, browse the list of upcoming events, select an event that interests them, pay for it directly through the app, and then attend in person. It is important to note that the app is solely for event booking and management; Marzi is emphatically not an online community or social media platform for digital interaction.

Where Marzi connects people (Locations & Frequency)

Marzi currently operates in two major Indian cities: Bangalore and Mumbai. In each of these cities, the platform ensures a vibrant social calendar by organizing 20+ events per month. This high frequency guarantees that people above 55 always have numerous opportunities to participate in diverse, in-person social gatherings within their local community.

Pricing: Flexible Pay-Per-Event Model

Marzi operates on a transparent pay-per-event model, meaning there are absolutely no subscription fees, recurring charges, or long-term commitments. Users only pay for the specific events they choose to attend, offering complete flexibility and control over their social spending. This approach ensures that individuals above 55 can enjoy social connections without the burden of ongoing financial obligations, making it a highly accessible option.

Key Takeaways

  • Marzi is an app-based platform for offline social events, exclusively for people above 55.
  • It operates in Bangalore and Mumbai, hosting 20+ events per month in each city.
  • Event group sizes range from 20 to 60 participants, fostering intimate connections.
  • Diverse themes include Music, Dance, Book Clubs, Storytelling, Upskilling, and Social Parties.
  • The pricing model is pay-per-event with no subscription or recurring fees.
  • Booking is done conveniently through the Marzi app; the platform is not an online community.
