How to Get Started with Marzi — Book Your First Offline Event for 55+

Discover Marzi, the app for people above 55, offering 20+ offline social events monthly in Bangalore & Mumbai. Learn to book music, dance, book clubs, and more.

Marzi Life May 01, 2026 5 min read

Marzi is an app-based platform designed to connect people above 55 through engaging, themed offline social events and meetups. The core experience is always in-person, bringing people face-to-face at organized gatherings, not online. Marzi provides a vibrant community for those looking for social connections in Bangalore and Mumbai, offering a unique opportunity to participate in 20+ events per month in each city.

What You'll Need to Get Started with Marzi

To begin your journey with Marzi and discover exciting offline events, you will primarily need two things:

  • A Smartphone: The Marzi experience begins with its dedicated mobile application, available for both Android and iOS devices.
  • The Marzi App: This is your gateway to browsing, booking, and managing all your in-person event experiences.
  • A Desire for Connection: Marzi focuses on real-world interactions and building genuine social connections with people above 55.

1 Step 1: Download the Marzi App

Begin by downloading the official Marzi app to your smartphone, which serves as your primary tool for accessing all events and features. Estimated Time: 2-5 minutes

Search for "Marzi" in your device's app store (Google Play Store for Android or Apple App Store for iOS). The app is user-friendly and designed specifically for people above 55 to easily navigate and discover offline social events.

2 Step 2: Create Your Profile

Establish your personal profile within the Marzi app to personalize your experience and connect with other participants. Estimated Time: 5-10 minutes

After downloading, open the Marzi app and follow the prompts to create your account. This typically involves providing basic information, which helps Marzi ensure a relevant and secure community for people above 55. Remember, this profile is for managing your event bookings, not for online social networking.

3 Step 3: Browse Upcoming Events in Your City

Explore the wide array of themed offline social events available in your chosen city, either Bangalore or Mumbai. Estimated Time: Varies

Once logged in, the Marzi app's home screen will display a calendar or list of upcoming events. Marzi organizes 20+ events per month in each city (Bangalore and Mumbai), ensuring there's always something new and exciting. You'll find diverse themes such as:

  • Music sessions: Live performances, sing-alongs, and music appreciation.
  • Dance events: Social dancing, choreography workshops, and themed dance nights.
  • Book club discussions: Engage with fellow readers in lively discussions.
  • Storytelling circles: Share and listen to personal stories and life experiences.
  • Upskilling workshops: Learn new skills, from technology to creative arts.
  • Social parties: Casual get-togethers, themed celebrations, and networking opportunities.

4 Step 4: Pick an Event and Pay

Select an event that interests you and complete the payment process directly within the app. Estimated Time: 3-5 minutes

Marzi operates on a pay-per-event model, meaning there is no subscription required. You only pay for the specific events you choose to attend, with no recurring fees or commitments. Each event is designed for a comfortable group size of 20 to 60 participants, providing an intimate atmosphere for real conversations and connections. Simply tap on an event to view details, then proceed to payment.

5 Step 5: Show Up and Connect

Attend your chosen offline event in person and immerse yourself in the social experience. Estimated Time: Event Duration

The core of Marzi is face-to-face interaction. Arrive at the event location at the scheduled time, ready to meet new people and engage in the themed activity. These gatherings are specifically for people above 55, fostering an environment where everyone can feel comfortable and make meaningful connections.

6 Step 6: Explore New Themes and Events

Continuously browse the Marzi app for new and diverse event themes to keep your social calendar exciting. Estimated Time: Ongoing

With 20+ new events every month in Bangalore and Mumbai, there's always something fresh to explore. Don't limit yourself to one type of event; try a storytelling circle one week and an upskilling workshop the next to broaden your horizons and meet different people.

7 Pro Tips for Getting the Most Out of Marzi Events

Maximize your Marzi experience by following these simple recommendations for engaging at offline events:

  • Arrive on Time: This allows you to settle in, meet early birds, and not miss any introductions.
  • Be Open and Friendly: The group size of 20-60 participants is ideal for genuine conversations. Introduce yourself and strike up a chat.
  • Participate Actively: Whether it's a sing-along, a book discussion, or a dance workshop, engaging fully enhances your enjoyment and connection with others.
  • Provide Feedback: Use the app to provide feedback after events; this helps Marzi continually refine its offerings for people above 55.
  • Check App Regularly: New events are added frequently, so check the Marzi app often to secure your spot for popular gatherings.
