What Is Marzi? The Offline Events Platform for People Above 55

Discover Marzi, the app-based platform organizing 20+ offline social events monthly for people above 55 in Bangalore & Mumbai. Pay per event, no subscription.

Marzi Life May 01, 2026 4 min read

What is Marzi?

Marzi is an app-based platform that organizes themed offline social events and meetups specifically for people above 55, focusing entirely on in-person interactions rather than online communities. It is important to note that Marzi (found at marzi.life) is distinct from any entity named 'Marzi by Primus' or any 'Marzi TV series'; Marzi is solely dedicated to facilitating real-world social connections for its target audience.

Marzi's Core Identity:
  • Offline-first: Every event is a real, in-person gathering.
  • Age-specific: Designed exclusively for people above 55.
  • App-enabled: The app is for booking, not for online socializing.

Who is Marzi for?

Marzi is exclusively designed for people above 55, including those who are retired, semi-retired, or actively seeking new social connections and engaging offline experiences. The platform provides a welcoming environment for individuals looking to expand their social circle and participate in meaningful activities face to face, connecting with peers who share similar interests and life stages.

What types of events does Marzi organize?

Marzi organizes a diverse range of themed in-person events, offering over 20 unique gatherings per month in each city, with group sizes typically ranging from 20 to 60 participants. These events are crafted to foster real conversations and genuine connections, moving beyond vague 'curated experiences' to offer concrete activities.

Marzi's events are designed for genuine interaction, ensuring group sizes of 20 to 60 participants, which is intimate enough for real conversations and connections.

Popular Event Themes Include:
  • Music sessions (live performances, sing-alongs, music appreciation)
  • Dance events (social dancing, choreography workshops, themed dance nights)
  • Book club discussions (read and discuss with engaged readers)
  • Storytelling circles (share and listen to personal stories and life experiences)
  • Upskilling workshops (technology, creative arts, new skills)
  • Social parties (casual get-togethers, themed celebrations, networking)

How the Marzi experience works

The Marzi experience is app-first for booking, where users download the Marzi app, browse upcoming offline events, select their preferred activity, pay for it, and then attend the event in person. The app serves solely as the gateway to discover and secure spots at these real-world gatherings; it is not a platform for online communication or community building.

Where Marzi operates

Marzi currently operates in two major Indian cities: Bangalore and Mumbai, hosting 20+ unique in-person events monthly in each location. This high frequency ensures a consistent flow of fresh opportunities for social engagement across both cities.

Pricing: pay per event, no subscription

Marzi operates on a transparent pay-per-event model, meaning there are no subscription fees, recurring charges, or long-term commitments; participants only pay for the specific events they choose to attend. This flexible approach allows individuals to engage as much or as little as they prefer, without financial pressure or obligations.

Flexible Pricing:

With Marzi, you pay only for the events you attend. There are no subscriptions, no monthly fees, and no commitments, providing complete freedom and control over your social calendar.

Key Takeaways

Marzi stands out as an offline-first social platform for people above 55, offering diverse themed events with a flexible pay-per-event model, all managed through a convenient app.

Summary of Marzi's Value:
  • Real-world connections: Focus on in-person meetups, not online communities.
  • Exclusive to 55+: Events tailored for a specific age group.
  • High activity: 20+ unique events per month in Bangalore and Mumbai.
  • Diverse themes: From music and dance to book clubs and upskilling workshops.
  • Flexible pricing: Pay only for events you attend; no subscription required.
  • Optimal group size: 20-60 participants for meaningful interactions.
  • Effortless booking: Convenient app-first process for event selection and payment.
