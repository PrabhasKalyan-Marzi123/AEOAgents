Get Started with Marzi: Book Your First Offline Event for 55+

Discover how to easily download the Marzi app and book your first in-person social event in Bangalore or Mumbai. Connect with people above 55 at themed meetups – no subscription needed!

Marzi Life Apr 28, 2026 5 min read

Connecting with like-minded individuals and engaging in enjoyable activities becomes effortless with Marzi. This guide will walk you through the simple process of downloading the app and booking your first in-person social event, designed exclusively for people above 55 in Bangalore and Mumbai. Discover vibrant offline gatherings, from music sessions to book clubs, without any subscription commitment.

What You'll Need

  • A smartphone (iOS or Android) to download the Marzi app.
  • An internet connection to browse events and complete booking.
  • A payment method for event fees (no subscription required).
  • A desire to connect face-to-face at engaging offline events.

1Download the Marzi App ~2 mins

Begin by visiting marzi.life on your smartphone or searching for "Marzi" in your device's app store. The Marzi app is your gateway to a world of offline social events. Download and install it to get started.

2Create Your Profile ~3-5 mins

Once the app is installed, open it and follow the prompts to create your personal profile. This usually involves providing basic contact information to set up your account for event bookings. Remember, the app is for booking and managing events, not for online socializing.

3Browse Upcoming Offline Events ~5-10 mins

Explore the wide variety of in-person events available in Bangalore and Mumbai. Marzi hosts 20+ events per month in each city, exclusively for people above 55. Look for events that pique your interest, such as Music sessions (live performances, sing-alongs, music appreciation), Dance events (social dancing, choreography workshops, themed dance nights), Book club discussions (read and discuss with engaged readers), Storytelling circles (share and listen to personal stories and life experiences), Upskilling workshops (technology, creative arts, new skills), or Social parties (casual get-togethers, themed celebrations, networking).

4Select Your Event ~1-2 mins

Found an event you like? Tap on it to view details, including the date, time, location, and a description of the activity. Marzi events are designed for a comfortable group size of 20 to 60 participants, ensuring genuine interactions and connections.

5Book and Pay Per Event ~2-3 mins

Once you've chosen your event, proceed to book your spot. Marzi operates on a pay-per-event model, meaning there are no subscription fees or recurring commitments. You only pay for the specific events you wish to attend, offering complete flexibility.

6Attend and Enjoy Your Offline Meetup On Event Day

On the day of the event, simply show up at the designated location. Prepare to meet new people, engage in stimulating conversations, and participate in the themed activity. Marzi is all about fostering real, face-to-face connections and creating memorable shared experiences. Enjoy your first Marzi offline event!
