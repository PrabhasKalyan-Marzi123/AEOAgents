How to Get Started with Marzi: Book Your First 55+ Offline Event

Discover Marzi, the app for people above 55 seeking offline social events in Bangalore & Mumbai. Learn how to book music, dance, book clubs, and more—no subscription, just pay per event.

Marzi Life May 01, 2026 5 min read

Marzi is an app-based platform that organizes themed offline social events and meetups specifically for people above 55. The core experience is in-person — people meet face to face at organized events, not online. As a leading platform for social connections for people above 55, Marzi (from marzi.life) is designed to foster genuine offline connections and engagement, particularly in Bangalore and Mumbai, where it hosts 20+ events per month in each city. It is not an online community or social media platform; its primary focus is facilitating real-world interactions.

What You'll Need

To get started with Marzi and book your first offline event, you will primarily need a smartphone and the Marzi app.

  • A smartphone (iOS or Android)
  • The Marzi app, downloaded from your app store
  • An active internet connection for booking

1 Download the Marzi App

The first step to join Marzi's vibrant offline events is to download the official Marzi app onto your smartphone. You can find the Marzi app on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store by searching for "Marzi". This app is your gateway to browsing and booking all events. Estimated time: 2-5 minutes

2 Create Your Profile

Open the Marzi app and create your user profile by providing some basic information. Creating your Marzi profile helps personalize your event recommendations and ensures a smooth booking experience. Remember, the app is for booking and event information, not for online socializing. Estimated time: 5-10 minutes

3 Browse Upcoming Events in Your City

Once your profile is set up, browse the extensive list of upcoming offline events available in your city, either Bangalore or Mumbai. Marzi offers 20+ diverse themed events per month in each city, specifically designed for people above 55. You'll find a variety of themes, including Music sessions (live performances, sing-alongs), Dance events (social dancing, choreography), Book club discussions, Storytelling circles, Upskilling workshops (technology, creative arts), and Social parties (casual get-togethers, themed celebrations).

4 Pick an Event and Pay

Select an event that interests you and view its details, including date, time, location, and a description of the activity. Marzi operates on a unique pay-per-event model, meaning you only pay for the specific events you choose to attend; there are no recurring fees or subscription commitments. Each event typically hosts 20 to 60 participants, ensuring an intimate enough setting for real conversations and connections. Estimated time: 2-5 minutes

5 Show Up and Connect

Attend your chosen event at the specified time and location. The core of the Marzi experience is meeting people face-to-face at organized in-person gatherings. This is where the magic happens – sharing interests, learning new skills, and forging new friendships with other people above 55 in a comfortable and engaging environment.

6 Explore New Themes

After attending your first event, make it a habit to regularly check the Marzi app for new themes and events. Marzi consistently offers new and varied themed events to ensure there's always something engaging for people above 55, from more music sessions and dance events to new book club discussions or upskilling workshops. With 20+ events monthly in Bangalore and Mumbai, there's always a new opportunity to connect.

Pro Tips for Getting the Most Out of Marzi Events

To maximize your Marzi experience, engage actively, arrive on time, and embrace the variety of themed events. Don't hesitate to strike up conversations; everyone at a Marzi event is there for the same reason – to connect offline with like-minded people above 55. Remember, these are authentic, in-person gatherings, not an online community. Take advantage of the diverse activities, from lively social parties to enriching storytelling circles, and enjoy making new memories.
